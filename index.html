1. How does real-time AI lip sync work in our platform?

Our AI analyzes audio input—whether it's voice-over or live speech—and automatically maps it to accurate lip movements on your 3D character in real time. This eliminates the need for manual keyframing or third-party animation software.

2. Can I customize the character animations and story responses?

Absolutely! While our AI handles the heavy lifting of animation and branching dialogue, you stay in full creative control. You can tweak expressions, gestures, pacing, and how characters react based on player choices using our easy-to-use interface.

3. Is this solution compatible with Unity or Unreal Engine?

Yes! We offer plug-and-play SDKs and APIs that integrate smoothly with both Unity and Unreal Engine. Whether you're building a game, simulation, or narrative experience, integration is seamless and developer-friendly.

4. What makes this different from traditional animation pipelines?

Traditional pipelines require manual lip-syncing, motion capture, and hours of rendering. Our AI automates all of that in real time—reducing production time from weeks to minutes. You can prototype, animate, and iterate faster than ever before..
